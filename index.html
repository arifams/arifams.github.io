Hi my name is Arif

I live in Amsterdam and currently own two beautiful gold fish and a harmonica. I love cooking, painting, meditating, coding, and playing ukulele. Oh yeah, I love summer.

I started my career as an art therapist by established a street kids house in 1996. With other care takers we were responsible for approximately 200 local kids and 30 volunteers on a daily basis program. We were helping the street kids to overcome their abusive trauma with an art class. It were really busy days but also some of the most awesome years in my life, unfortunately due to the local political situation we could only do it for 3 years.

After that I became a teacher, marine, app developer, animator, model, skateboarder, and even a model. During this time, cooking became one of my favourite hobby's. I took my cooking hobby to another level by opening a small shop to provide breakfast and lunch. One of my best seller dishes was coconut rice with soy sauce tofu stew. In 2002, my shop had 250 clients for breakfast and it's double during the lunch time.

Then, I decide to travel around the world.

After I'm done with my chef education and JavaScript/Ruby training, I do some travel again to visit the kitchens of my friends and family, to learn the wisdom from their kitchen. At the same time I am building an app to collect traditional and local recipes. You can read about it further in my Project section. Anyway, welcome to my site :)

Recent Projects

Project 15 Kitchen - To connect with real people by learning how they cook, treat their meal and share the food and recipes to another kitchen. (stack: HTML, CSS, JQuery, Bootstrap, Socmed: Youtube+Instagram)

FDLV - An app to support and collect the recipes during 15kitchen projects and share it among it's users. Status: on progress, working prototype. (stack: Ruby on Rails)

Prongpong - An eco village project based in Sumenep, Madura. Working together with a group of 4 farmers (a group approximately consist of 20 farmers), a local school (with approximately 300 students and 60 teachers), a boarding school (40 kids and 6 teachers) and 3 environmentalist experts. The goal is to reduce the use of chemical fertilizers and move on to organic farming, to encourage the local kids to keep their environment by planting trees and mangroves around their village and to establish a sustainable business case. My role is developing a web app, building the business model plan and be a communication facilitator for all the stakeholders. (stack: Google Docs, WhatsApp, Slack, PHP, JQuery, Wordpress)

International People's Tribunal 1965 - To support a working group which is focused to human rights and international law affairs campaignes. (stack: adobe CC, PHP, Wordpress, security and QA test tools)
